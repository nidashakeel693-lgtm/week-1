Who Are WeatherWalay?

WeatherWalay is a registered company working towards a "state-of-the-art" weather forecasting system in Pakistan. The WeatherWalay team is working hard to address Pakistan's problems related to weather. This will help many businesses across Pakistan to improve their productivity and profitability. The WeatherWalay nowcasts and forecasts are significantly improved to help the public and businesses make timely and accurate predictions through weather data analysis.

Dr. Muhammad Hanif

Advisory Board Member

Dr. Hanif is a seasoned meteorologist with 24+ years at the Pakistan Meteorological Department, where he served as Chief Meteorologist. A specialist in operational meteorology and hydrology, he has led national and regional initiatives on climate adaptation, early warning systems, and disaster risk reduction.

Major General Fida Hussain Malik (Retd)

Advisory Board Member

Major General Fida Hussain Malik (Retd), HI(M) is a decorated Pakistan Army officer with 37 years of distinguished service in command, logistics, and multinational peacekeeping operations. A respected author and strategic thinker, he now spearheads the Green Climate Alliance, championing sustainability and environmental resilience.
